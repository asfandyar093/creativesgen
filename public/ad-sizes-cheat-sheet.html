Facebook & Instagram Ad Sizes

Meta Ads Manager supports these placements. Use the same creative across placements by generating all sizes at once.

DimensionsPlacementAspect RatioNotes
1080 × 1080 pxTop Pick Feed — Square 1:1 Works on Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Marketplace. Most versatile size.
1200 × 628 px Feed — Landscape 1.91:1 Standard landscape. Best for link ads and single image ads on Facebook desktop.
1080 × 1350 px Feed — Portrait 4:5 Takes up more vertical space in Feed. Often outperforms square for attention capture.
1080 × 1920 pxTop Pick Stories & Reels 9:16 Full-screen vertical. Required for Instagram Stories and Reels ads. Safe zone: keep key elements in center 1080×1420 px.
1080 × 1080 px Carousel — Each Slide 1:1 Each carousel card is square. Minimum 2 cards, maximum 10 cards per carousel.
600 × 600 px (min) Audience Network 1:1 Minimum accepted. Recommend 1080×1080 for quality.

Google Display Network Ad Sizes

The GDN shows ads across 2+ million websites. These 6 sizes cover 90%+ of available impressions.

DimensionsNameCoverageNotes
300 × 250 pxTop Pick Medium Rectangle Highest reach Single highest-traffic placement on the GDN. Appears inline in content and sidebars. Always create this size.
728 × 90 pxTop Pick Leaderboard Very high reach Top and bottom of page. Second most common placement. High visibility on desktop.
300 × 600 px Half Page / Large Rectangle High reach Large format with strong visual impact. Often appears in sidebars. Good CPM efficiency.
160 × 600 px Wide Skyscraper Medium reach Tall sidebar placement. Less common than leaderboard but fills placements that 300px-wide ads can't.
970 × 250 px Billboard Medium reach Large horizontal format. Appears at top of page on premium publisher sites. High impact.
320 × 50 pxTop Pick Mobile Banner High mobile reach Essential for mobile campaigns. Appears at top/bottom of app screens. Mobile-only placement.
320 × 100 px Large Mobile Banner Medium mobile reach 2x taller than standard mobile banner. More space for messaging without going full interstitial.
468 × 60 px Banner Lower reach Older format, lower inventory than leaderboard. Still used on some legacy publisher sites.
File specs: Max file size 150 KB for static images. Accepted formats: PNG, JPG, GIF (static only for most placements). For animated HTML5 ads, use Google Web Designer. For responsive display ads, upload 3–4 images and let Google auto-size.

TikTok Ad Sizes

TikTok is a video-first platform — most TikTok ads are video. For image ads (TopView static, Spark Ads), use these specs.

DimensionsFormatAspect RatioNotes
1080 × 1920 pxPrimary In-Feed / TopView Video 9:16 Full screen vertical video. Standard TikTok ad format. Keep safe zone: top 130px and bottom 250px for UI overlay.
1080 × 1080 px In-Feed Square 1:1 Square video or image. Slightly lower CTR than vertical on TikTok, but reusable from Meta campaigns.
1080 × 1920 px TopView Static Image 9:16 Static image for TopView placement. Appears when users open the app. High visibility, premium placement.
800 × 800 px (min) TikTok Shop Ad 1:1 Product catalog images for TikTok Shopping. Plain white background recommended. Show product clearly.
TikTok tip: TikTok rewards native-looking content. Ads that look like organic TikToks — shot vertically, with captions, trending audio — consistently outperform polished brand ads. Your 9:16 creative from Instagram Stories can often work here too.

LinkedIn Ad Sizes

LinkedIn ads reach B2B decision-makers. Single Image Ads in the feed are the most common format.

DimensionsFormatAspect RatioNotes
1200 × 627 pxPrimary Single Image — Landscape 1.91:1 Most common LinkedIn ad format. Appears in desktop and mobile feed. Same dimensions as Facebook landscape.
1200 × 1200 px Single Image — Square 1:1 Takes more vertical space in feed. Often performs better on mobile where square creatives fill more screen.
628 × 1200 px Single Image — Portrait 1:1.91 Vertical format. Maximum feed real estate on mobile. Growing in popularity for B2B campaigns.
1200 × 627 px per card Carousel — Each Card 1.91:1 2–10 cards per carousel. Each card can have a different headline and destination URL.
100 × 100 px (min) Sponsored Messaging Logo 1:1 Company logo for Message Ads and Conversation Ads. Recommend 300×300 for quality.
LinkedIn tip: LinkedIn users are in a professional mindset. Lead with a clear value proposition and data. "Reduce CPA by 40%" outperforms "beautiful visuals" on this platform. Keep text-to-image ratio moderate — LinkedIn allows more text overlay than Meta.
