Pricing FAQ

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?
No. You can create an account and generate 15 images completely free with no credit card required. Your account data is stored locally on your device.
Does one plan cover all three tools?
Yes. Your image count is shared across all tools — Social Posts, Ad Creatives, and Website Images. Generating 1 social post image and 1 ad image both count as 1 image each toward your monthly total.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel any time and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?
When you reach your monthly image limit, you'll be prompted to upgrade. Images don't roll over to the next month. Enterprise plans can include custom higher limits.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational use?
Yes, we offer 50% discounts for registered non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact us at hello@creativesgen.com with proof of status.
