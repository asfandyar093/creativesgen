FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Creatives Gen.

What AI model generates the images?
Creatives Gen uses Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash image generation model by default, with options to use Imagen 4 for higher quality output. The text planning uses Gemini 2.5 Flash for fast, accurate copy generation.
How long does it take to generate an image?
Most images are generated in 10–30 seconds. Carousel posts with multiple slides may take 1–2 minutes total as each slide is generated individually for consistency.
Can I use my brand colors and logo?
Yes. You can upload your logo, pick brand colors using a color picker, extract colors from an uploaded image, or input hex codes directly. Your brand settings are remembered between sessions.
What formats and sizes are supported?
Social Posts: 1:1, 4:5, 9:16. Ad Creatives: 3 Meta sizes and 6 Google Display sizes. Website Images: 5 formats including 16:9, 1:1, 4:5, 9:16, and 21:9 ultra-wide.
What happens after my 15 free images?
You can upgrade to a paid plan starting at $19/month for 200 images. Plans work across all three tools — social, ads, and website images. No credit card is needed for the free trial.
Can I download the images in high resolution?
Yes. All generated images are downloaded at full AI output resolution (typically 1024px or higher). You can download individual images or all images at once as a ZIP file.
