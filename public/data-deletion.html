Data & Privacy

Data Deletion Request

Last updated: April 2025

How to Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of all personal data associated with your Creatives Gen account at any time. This includes your profile information, usage history, and any preferences stored in our system.

To submit a deletion request, email us at:

✉️ support@creativesgen.com
Subject line: Data Deletion Request
Include: The email address or name associated with your account.

What Gets Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, we will permanently remove:

  • Your name, email address, and profile photo
  • Login provider information (Google / Facebook)
  • Image generation usage history
  • Subscription and plan data
  • Any brand preferences or settings stored in your account

Timeline

We will process your request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Note: If you signed in via Facebook or Google, revoking app permissions from those platforms will also prevent future logins. You can do this in your Facebook or Google account security settings.

Revoke App Access

You can also revoke Creatives Gen's access directly from your social accounts:

  • Facebook: Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites → Remove Creatives Gen
  • Google: myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party apps → Remove Creatives Gen
