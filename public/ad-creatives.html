FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating ad creatives with Creatives Gen.

Can I generate all ad sizes in one click? ▼
Yes. Select all the sizes you need using the checkboxes — Meta Feed, Stories, and multiple Google Display sizes — then click Generate. All selected sizes are created simultaneously from your single product description and brand settings.
Does the AI write the ad copy for me? ▼
You can provide your own headline, body copy, and CTA — or leave any field blank and the AI fills it in using the AIDA framework optimized for your campaign objective and target audience. You're always in full control.
Are the generated ads ready to upload to Meta Ads Manager? ▼
Yes. Images are exported at the correct pixel dimensions for each placement. Download as individual files or a ZIP bundle and upload directly to Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, or any DSP.
How do I make a specific ad size look different? ▼
Click the "Edit" button on any generated ad image to select it, open the AI Chat panel, and type your changes. The AI applies your edit instruction to that specific image and regenerates it — without affecting the other sizes.
Can I test multiple creative variations? ▼
Absolutely. Generate one set of creatives, then modify the style, color palette, or copy direction and generate another set. Each session is saved in your chat history so you can compare variations side by side.
