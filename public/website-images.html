FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about generating website images with Creatives Gen.

What website section types are supported? ▼
Creatives Gen supports all common website sections: hero, features, about, testimonials, pricing, blog headers, contact, and footer areas. You can also choose "Other" and describe any custom section type — the AI will create appropriate imagery for any web context you describe.
Will the images contain text or be clean backgrounds? ▼
By default, Creatives Gen generates clean images without text overlaid — so you can add your own typography on top in your website builder. You can specify in the content field whether you want text visible in the image or a clean visual-only design.
Can I generate images in multiple sizes at once? ▼
Yes. Select multiple format checkboxes (16:9, 4:3, 1:1, portrait, etc.) and set a quantity per format. Creatives Gen generates all selected format combinations simultaneously — giving you a complete image set for your section in one generation.
Are the images high enough resolution for retina displays? ▼
Generated images are high-resolution PNG files suitable for retina/HiDPI displays. For hero banners and full-width sections we recommend using them at 1:1 scale or verifying they meet your specific page's maximum display width before publishing.
Can I use generated images commercially on client websites? ▼
Yes. You own the images you generate with Creatives Gen and can use them for commercial purposes, including client projects. Review our Terms of Service for the full details on content ownership and usage rights.
How do I match the style of my existing website? ▼
Enter your brand colors in the brand settings panel and describe your existing visual style in the "Visual Style" field (e.g. "clean minimal, lots of white space, soft gradients"). You can also upload a screenshot of your existing site as a reference image to guide the AI's visual direction.
