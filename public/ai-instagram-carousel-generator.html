FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about generating social media posts with Creatives Gen.

How many slides can I generate per carousel? ▼
You can generate between 3 and 10 slides per carousel. The AI plans each slide individually with its own visual direction and copy, so every slide is purposefully designed — not just copied from a template.
Can I edit the AI-generated copy before creating images? ▼
Yes — that's the whole point of the two-step flow. The AI first presents a slide-by-slide copy plan. You can edit any slide's headline, body text, or CTA before a single image is generated. You're always in control of the messaging.
What file format are the generated images? ▼
All images are generated as high-quality PNG or JPEG files. You can download each image individually with one click, or download all slides as a ZIP bundle — ready to upload directly to any social platform.
Can I use my own logo and brand colors? ▼
Absolutely. Enter your brand name, hex color codes, and upload your logo in the brand settings panel. The AI automatically applies your brand identity to every generated image. You can also upload a reference image to guide the visual style.
How do I edit an image after it's been generated? ▼
Click the "Edit" button on any generated image to select it, then open the AI Chat panel. Type your edit request — "make the background darker", "change the color to blue", "add more energy to this slide" — and the AI regenerates that specific image with your changes applied.
Does Creatives Gen work for video content? ▼
Creatives Gen currently generates static images optimized for social media carousels and posts. We're working on animated and video features for a future release. In the meantime, you can use the generated slides as frames in video editing tools like CapCut or Canva.
